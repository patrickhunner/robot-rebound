
# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Robot Rebound is a multiplayer puzzle game.

## Information We Collect

When played through Discord, Robot Rebound may receive limited information from Discord necessary to operate the game, including:

- Discord user ID
- Discord display name
- Discord avatar
- Discord Activity instance information

We do not collect passwords or payment information.

## How Information Is Used

This information is used only to:

- identify players during a game
- display player names and avatars
- synchronize multiplayer sessions

## Data Storage

Game state is stored only as long as necessary to operate the current game session.

We do not intentionally maintain long-term player profiles or gameplay history.

## Sharing

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties.

## Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect transmitted information, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

Robot Rebound is not specifically directed toward children under 13.
